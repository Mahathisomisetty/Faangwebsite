Our Story



Seeing many MS students struggle to find their first job in the USA made me realize something important. Many of us weren't properly prepared before stepping into the real world. That's whenI had an idea: why not teach and guide engineering students the right way before they graduate? Why not give them the skills and confidence they need to succeed? That thought and idea made me to come back to India and dedicate myself to this mission. It hasn't been easy, but we're making it happen. When it started it's just "me, "now we've grown into a team of 70 passionate people at Faang Tech Lab. This is my way of giving back, and it fills my heart every single day.
